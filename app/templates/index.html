INFORMATION

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) a type of Artificial Neural Network, where the model will learn to find a hidden patterns or structures that are difficult for eye to see. The model will then classify images based on the information learned through training process of the model. CNN uses a system much like a multilayer perceptron that has been designed for reduced processing requirements. The layers of a CNN consist of an input layer, an output layer and a hidden layer that includes multiple convolutional layers, pooling layers, fully connected layers and normalization layers

Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) is a Deep-Learning-based generative model that can be used to generate a new data based on a pre-trained set of data. The GAN model architecture involves two sub-models: a generator model for generating new examples and a discriminator model for classifying whether generated examples are real, from the domain, or fake, generated by the generator model.

A brain tumour is a growth of cells in the brain that multiplies in an abnormal, uncontrollable way. Brain tumors can be cancerous (malignant) or noncancerous (benign). When benign or malignant tumors grow, they can cause the pressure inside your skull to increase. This can cause brain damage, and it can be life-threatening.
